01Executive Summary

Consolidated from 7 independent analyses into a unified 6-month execution plan.

This roadmap synthesizes findings from a training maturity assessment, two design audits, a strategic analysis framework, a full expansion development spec, and two phased HTML expansion plans. It defines a path from TMMi Level 2 (Managed) to Level 4 (Measured) across four parallel workstreams.

The platform has strong content foundations — 15 well-written modules, dual-audience design, CARD prompting framework, and HITL governance. The biggest gaps are in platform UX (13 design findings), assessment rigor (only 33% exercise coverage), and Playwright capability coverage (functional testing only — no visual, a11y, performance, or component testing).
